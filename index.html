About Me

I have taken quite a journey to discover my passion for programming. Always having an affinity and an aptitude for "computery" things—building PCs in my youth, becoming a power-user of every platform and application I came across—I had just considered it a hobby. In fact, my higher education is in a mostly unrelated field, Music.

Persuing, and acquiring, a Master's degree took me to New York City, where I lived and worked for almost 20 years. As a young musician, I supplimented my income through work in the financial services industry. Over time, I eventually found this field to be significantly more lucrative, waking up one day to realize it had become a full-fledged career. For 15 years I worked in desktop publishing and graphic design departments at preiminent financial firms in NYC, starting as an entry-level word processor, adding graphic design, becoming a trainer, and then ulimately an operations manager.
